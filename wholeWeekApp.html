About Whole Week

Whole Week was created for people with bad eating habits.

You can beat every bad habit but happy thoughts and will power will help only for short time. Whole Week app will help you build discipline if you are ready to beat this bad habit.

Philosophy

Why Whole Week?

The main idea is to avoid to much time in grocery stores. More you visit shops more you buy junk foods. If you could plan just one whole week - shop visits would drop down to one or two times in week and at home you would have only ingredients for planned meals.

whole week

What is Whole Week app?

Whole Week is your planning assistant and consists of three parts - Shopping List - Week Planer - Cook Book. The best way to use app is - add recipes to Cook Book, from Cook Book you can add to Week Planner next weeks meals. When your week is planned you can add all week ingedients to one Shopping List.
